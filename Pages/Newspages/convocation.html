    38th Annual Convocation Ceremony and Sports Day

      

    The 38th Annual Convocation Ceremony held on August 19, 2023, at the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore, marked a significant milestone in the institution's history. This momentous event brought together students, faculty, staff, families, and distinguished guests to celebrate the academic achievements and aspirations of a new generation of graduates.

    The presence of the Honourable Chancellor, Dr. G. Viswanathan, at the convocation added a profound sense of prestige and significance to the occasion. Dr. Vishwanathan, a visionary leader and guiding force behind VIT's journey to excellence, epitomizes the institution's commitment to education, innovation, and nation-building. His inspiring words and wisdom have long served as a source of motivation for both the academic community and students.

    The convocation ceremony was not only a time to confer degrees but also an opportunity to recognize and celebrate the hard work, dedication, and intellectual growth of the graduating class. It was a moment of pride for both the graduates and their families, as years of academic pursuit and personal development culminated in the receipt of their well-earned diplomas and degrees.

    During the ceremony, students were awarded degrees in various disciplines, reflecting the diverse talents and interests of the graduating class. The valedictorian's address resonated with hope and optimism for the future, inspiring the newly minted graduates to embark on their respective journeys with courage and determination.

    The 38th Annual Convocation Ceremony at VIT was not just an event; it was a symbol of the institution's commitment to shaping the leaders and innovators of tomorrow. It underscored VIT's dedication to academic excellence, research, and holistic development, preparing its graduates to excel in a rapidly changing world.
